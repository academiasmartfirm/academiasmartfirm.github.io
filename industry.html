Patent Licensing and Commercialization

We help universities activate and monetize existing patent portfolios when internal capacity or market reach is limited. Our process is evidence-driven and designed to generate tangible returns.

  • Structured portfolio review led by subject-matter experts with academic and industry experience.
  • Recommendations on which patents to retain and protect, license actively, or release.
  • Strategic positioning toward industry segments with realistic commercial uptake.
  • Direct outreach to industry partners to explore licensing opportunities.
  • Support through negotiation and structuring focused on sustainable income.
Proven outcomes. This approach has been implemented multiple times, generating licensing agreements and direct income for partner universities.
